Private Dining

Perfect for your next sporting event, birthday, corporate event, or wedding. Samantha’s Tap Room & Wood Grill offers a contemporary private dining room located on the mezzanine level just above the main dining. With large modern windows spanning across its corner walls, the private dining room allows an open feel while overlooking beautiful Main Street of downtown Little Rock. The space is ideal for business meetings, celebrations, reunions, and rehearsal dinners. Samantha’s private dining team is always on hand to assist in carefully planning any special event.
